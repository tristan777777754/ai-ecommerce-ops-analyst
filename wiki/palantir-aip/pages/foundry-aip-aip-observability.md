# AIP observability

AIP observability refers to a set of capabilities in [Workflow Lineage](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/workflow-lineage/overview/) that provide visibility into your AIP and Ontology workflow executions, including [metrics](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/aip-observability/metrics/), [execution history](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/aip-observability/run-history/), [distributed tracing](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/aip-observability/trace-view/), [logging](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/aip-observability/service-logs-and-debugging/), and [log search](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/aip-observability/log-search/). These capabilities are part of Palantir's [platform-wide observability](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/observability/overview/) strategy.

To learn more about available tools and how to get started, see the [AIP observability section](https://www.palantir.com/docs/foundry/aip-observability/overview/).
